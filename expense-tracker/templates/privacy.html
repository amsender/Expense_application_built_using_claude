Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly collects when you use our personal expense tracking application (the "Service"), how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide the Service to you. This includes:

  • Account information — such as your name and email address, provided when you register.
  • Expense data — the transactions you record, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.
  • Basic usage information — technical details such as your browser type, needed to keep the Service working correctly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect solely to operate and improve the Service. This includes:

  • Displaying your expenses, summaries, and spending reports.
  • Authenticating you and keeping your account secure.
  • Maintaining, troubleshooting, and improving the Service.

We do not sell your personal financial data, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored so that it is available to you whenever you use the Service. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on a limited number of trusted third-party services to operate — for example, for hosting or fonts. These providers only receive the information necessary to perform their function and are expected to safeguard it.

We do not share your personal expense data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will do our best to respond promptly.
